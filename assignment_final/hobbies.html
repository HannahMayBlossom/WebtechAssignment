Interest and Hobbies

Interests

Coding

Naturally, I have an interest in coding and persue this outside my university time. Outside my lectures I've been teaching myself aspects as well as, getting tips from people I know. My interest in the subject grew when I started playing video games and wanted to learn more and build my own pc. While I never had the money to do so, I tried to do my own research so I could buy the best equipment I could. Also, as the sole child in the household it fell to me to solve all technical issues so I had to learn how to be Tech-Support too. The moment I choose to fully immerse myself in the subject, was when a friend was struggling with Java and bounced ideas off of me and I found that I understood and was interested in learning more. I thoroughly enjoyed learning what I did in my own and when I asked a friend to teach me more, I was told about my course so I took a chance and jumped on it. I am glad I did and I can't wait to continue to learn and grow in the subject.
I can't wait to get started on some projects! As soon as I have finished my course however.

Creative Writing

What drove me to choosing to do English originally was a fondness for writing stories. When I realised I was good as Linguistics as well, I choose to do it as an undergraduate course. I started writing FanFiction as a way of escapism from when I was a teenager, reading my old work I know I wasn't any good but I kept going and now I am a lot better. I love being creative, and I now write for others and it brings me joy when other people read my work and are happy. On various sites, I get requests for certain "Fandoms" and practise my writing this way. I even prepare for writing assignments this way too! Eventually, like all young writers, I turned my hand to writing novels. I love creating characters and interesting worlds for them to live in. I write for pretty much every genre but I prefer writing Young Adult, the genre that inspired me so much. I have written multiple novels, short stories and novella's but the thing I am most proud of is writing a trilogy.

Hobbies

As mentioned, one of my favourite hobbies is naturally, playing video games and when I was younger, I wanted to be able to develop games and write for them. While, that job would be amazing, I know I can make a difference elsewhere. I still love gaming and while I'm not exactly the best at it, or a harcore tryhard, I still try and play as many games as I can.
I also love swimming, and used to swim a lot and recently, I have been able to go back to swimming and rekindle that love. It takes my mind off problems and I am always able to come up with solutions to codes or beat troublesome bosses after a good swim
Walking and visiting historical places are another thing I like to do. I enjoy going to castles and walking around gardens, I've always had a passion for history and even volunteered to archive literature at my university library. Now that I live in Wales I've tried my hardest to take in the sites and gardens. It's a beautiful country and I want to see more of it.
I am also an amature baker, I adore baking and cooking. I am very much a try it and see cook, while I read recipes to get ideas I don't often follow them. Baking is a form of stress relief for me too, so I tend to bake often around assignment time much to the joy of everyone around me.
